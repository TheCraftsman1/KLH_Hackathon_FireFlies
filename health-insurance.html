What is Health Insurance?

Health insurance covers your medical and surgical expenses arising from illness or injury. A good health plan ensures you receive quality healthcare without worrying about the financial burden of rising medical costs.

With medical inflation at 14% per year in India, a ₹5 Lakh hospital bill today would cost ₹19 Lakh in 10 years. Health insurance protects your savings from being wiped out by a single medical emergency.

What's Covered

  • AYUSH Treatment: Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy covered

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the waiting period?
Most health insurance plans have an initial waiting period of 30 days (except accidents), and pre-existing conditions are covered after 2-4 years depending on the insurer.
Can I add my family to my policy?
Yes! A family floater plan covers you, your spouse, and up to 4 children under a single sum insured. This is more cost-effective than individual policies.
How does cashless hospitalization work?
At any network hospital, you just show your health card. The insurer settles the bill directly with the hospital — you don't have to pay and claim later.
Are pre-existing conditions covered?
Yes, after a waiting period of 2-4 years. Some plans offer reduced waiting periods for pre-existing conditions at a slightly higher premium.
