What is Personal Accident Insurance?

Personal Accident Insurance provides financial protection against unforeseen accidents that may result in death, permanent/temporary disability, or bodily injury. It covers accidental death, permanent total/partial disability, and temporary total disability with weekly income replacement.

Unlike health insurance (which covers illness), PA insurance specifically covers accident-related expenses including hospitalization, fractures, burns, and ambulance charges.

Key Benefits

  • Accidental Death Benefit: Lump sum payout to nominee up to ₹1 Crore
  • Permanent Disability: 100% sum assured for permanent total disability
  • Temporary Disability: Weekly income benefit during recovery period
  • Medical Expenses: Covers hospitalization costs due to accidents
  • Worldwide Coverage: Protection wherever you go
  • Child Education: Some plans cover children's education if parent dies

FAQs

Is PA insurance different from health insurance?
Yes. Health insurance covers hospitalization due to illness and accidents, while PA insurance specifically covers accidental death, disability, and related expenses. Both complement each other and are recommended together.
Does PA insurance cover road accidents?
Yes, PA insurance covers all types of accidents including road accidents, falls, burns, drowning, animal bites, and any bodily injury caused by external, violent, and visible means.
Can I buy PA insurance for my family?
Yes, most insurers offer family PA plans that can cover you, your spouse, children, and parents under a single policy at a discounted premium.
