Why You Need a Retirement Plan

With increasing life expectancy (75+ years), you could spend 20-30 years in retirement. Without a steady income, your savings can deplete fast — especially with medical inflation at 14% per year.

A retirement plan ensures you receive a guaranteed monthly pension for as long as you live. Start early: investing ₹10K/month from age 30 can build a corpus of ₹2 Crore+ by age 60.

FAQs

When should I start planning for retirement?
Ideally in your 20s or 30s. Starting at 25 with ₹10K/month vs. 35 with ₹10K/month results in nearly 2x more corpus at 60, thanks to compounding.
What is NPS (National Pension System)?
NPS is a government-backed retirement scheme with low-cost fund management and extra tax benefits of ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B). You can invest in equity, corporate bonds, or government securities.
How much pension can I expect?
It depends on your corpus and annuity rate. Broadly, a ₹1 Crore corpus can generate ₹40,000-₹60,000/month pension depending on the annuity plan chosen.
