FAQs

Are LIC plans safe?
Yes, LIC plans are backed by the Government of India, making them one of the safest investment-cum-insurance options available. LIC has a sovereign guarantee on all its policies.
Can I buy LIC plans online?
Yes, many LIC plans like Tech Term, Jeevan Anand, and SIIP can be purchased online through LIC's website or through authorized platforms like Insurix.India.
What is the minimum age to buy LIC plans?
Most LIC plans can be purchased from age 18 onwards. Some child plans can be taken for children as young as 0 years (with parent as proposer).
