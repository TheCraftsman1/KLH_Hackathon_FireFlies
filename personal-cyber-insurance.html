Why Personal Cyber Insurance?

FAQs

Does cyber insurance cover UPI fraud?
Yes, personal cyber insurance covers financial losses from unauthorized UPI transactions, provided you report the fraud within the specified time (usually 24-48 hours) and file a police complaint/cyber complaint.
Is cyber insurance different from bank fraud protection?
Yes, while banks may refund unauthorized transactions in some cases, cyber insurance provides broader coverage including identity theft, cyber stalking, ransomware, social media hacking, and legal expenses — all of which banks don't cover.
How do I file a cyber insurance claim?
Report the cyber incident to the insurer's helpline, file a complaint on the cybercrime portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or local police, and submit supporting documents like bank statements, screenshots, and FIR copy. Most claims are settled within 15-30 days.
