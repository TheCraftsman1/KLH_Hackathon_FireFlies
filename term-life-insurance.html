What is Term Life Insurance?

Term life insurance is a pure protection plan that provides financial coverage to your family in case of the policyholder's unfortunate demise during the policy term. Unlike other life insurance policies, term plans offer the highest coverage at the lowest premium.

It ensures your family can maintain their lifestyle, pay off debts, fund children's education, and meet daily expenses even in your absence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who should buy term life insurance?
Anyone with financial dependents should buy term insurance. It is especially important for breadwinners, new parents, homeowners with loans, and anyone whose family would face financial hardship in their absence.
What is the ideal coverage amount?
Financial experts recommend coverage of 10-15 times your annual income. For example, if you earn ₹10 Lakh/year, you should aim for ₹1 Crore to ₹1.5 Crore coverage.
At what age should I buy term insurance?
The earlier, the better! Buying in your 20s means significantly lower premiums locked in for the entire term. A 25-year-old pays nearly 50% less than a 35-year-old for the same coverage.
Is the payout taxable?
No, the death benefit received by the nominee is completely tax-free under Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act.
