What are Guaranteed Return Plans?

Guaranteed Return Plans (also called Guaranteed Savings or Non-Participating plans) are life insurance policies that offer a fixed, guaranteed return regardless of market conditions. Your money grows at a predetermined rate — zero risk, zero market dependency.

These are ideal for risk-averse investors, senior citizens, or anyone looking for a safe alternative to Fixed Deposits with better returns and tax benefits.

Key Benefits

  • Guaranteed Returns: Fixed 6-7.5% p.a. return announced at purchase — will never change
  • No Market Risk: Returns are not linked to stock market or fund performance
  • Life Cover: Built-in life insurance cover for the policy term
  • Tax Benefits: Premium deductible under 80C, maturity tax-free under 10(10D)
  • Income Option: Choose lump sum or guaranteed monthly income at maturity
  • Better than FD: Higher effective returns than bank Fixed Deposits after tax

FAQs

How is it different from FD?
Unlike FDs, guaranteed plans offer tax-free maturity (no TDS), built-in life cover, and often higher effective returns (post-tax). FD interest is fully taxable at your slab rate.
Is there a lock-in period?
Yes, typically 5-10 years of premium paying term and 10-25 years of policy term. Early withdrawal carries surrender charges and lower returns.
Can I get monthly income from these plans?
Yes! Many plans offer a guaranteed monthly/annual income option after the premium paying term ends, which can serve as a second income or pension supplement.
