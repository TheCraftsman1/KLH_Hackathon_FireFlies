What's Covered in Two Wheeler Insurance?

Understand what your policy protects

Comprehensive Coverage

  • Damage due to accident/collision
  • Theft of your two wheeler
  • Fire and explosion
  • Natural calamities (flood, cyclone)
  • riots, strikes, malicious damage
  • Third party liability coverage
  • Personal accident cover

Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Electrical/mechanical breakdown
  • Driving without valid license
  • Driving under influence of alcohol
  • Intentional damage
  • Usage outside geographical area
  • Consequential losses
