FAQs

Can homemakers buy term insurance?
Yes! Several insurers offer term plans for homemakers with coverage up to ₹50 Lakh - ₹1 Crore, recognizing the economic value of household work.
Why are premiums lower for women?
Women statistically have a higher life expectancy than men in India (by ~3 years), which makes them a lower risk for insurers, resulting in lower premiums.
Is pregnancy covered?
Term insurance doesn't cover pregnancy directly (it's not an illness), but some plans offer premium waiver during pregnancy complications and maternity-related critical illness riders.
