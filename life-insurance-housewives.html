Why Do Housewives Need Life Insurance?

Homemakers contribute enormously to the family — from childcare and cooking to household management. If something happens to a homemaker, the family would need to hire help for all these tasks, costing lakhs per year. Life insurance for housewives protects the family from this financial burden.

Many insurers now offer term plans specifically for non-earning women, with no income proof required and premiums up to 20% lower than male counterparts.

FAQs

Can a housewife buy term insurance without income proof?
Yes! Several insurers offer term plans for housewives/homemakers without requiring income proof. The sum assured may be limited to ₹25-50 lakhs, but the coverage is genuine and claims are settled normally.
What is the maximum cover a housewife can get?
Most insurers offer up to ₹50 lakhs cover for housewives without income proof. If the husband's income is high, some insurers may offer higher coverage based on the family's financial profile.
Is the premium really lower for women?
Yes, women generally get 15-20% lower premiums compared to men of the same age. This is because women have a higher average life expectancy in India.
