Why Pet Insurance?

Veterinary costs in India have risen significantly, with surgeries costing ₹20,000-₹2,00,000 and cancer treatments even more. Pet insurance helps you give your pet the best medical care without worrying about costs.

FAQs

What pets can be insured?
Most pet insurance plans in India cover dogs and cats of all breeds. Some insurers also cover exotic pets. Pets typically need to be between 8 weeks and 8-10 years old at the time of enrollment.
Are pre-existing conditions covered?
Similar to human health insurance, pre-existing conditions are usually not covered immediately. There's typically a waiting period. However, some plans may cover them after a continuous coverage period of 12-24 months.
Is vaccination covered under pet insurance?
Some comprehensive plans include vaccination and preventive care as add-ons. Basic plans usually cover illness and accident treatment only. Check the specific plan details for preventive care coverage.
